# Refund Policy

Customers may request a refund within 30 days of purchase. Refunds for purchases
over 500 USD require manager approval. Refunds for amounts over 5,000 USD
require CFO approval.

Refunds are processed back to the original payment method within 5 business days
of approval. Cash refunds are not available.

EU customers retain their statutory 14-day right of withdrawal in addition to
this policy.
