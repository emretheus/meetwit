# Discount Policy

Standard discount is 10 percent off list price for qualified customers.

The CFO must approve any discount above 15 percent. Discounts above 25 percent
are not permitted under any circumstances — these require executive committee
review and a documented business case.

Volume discounts kick in at 100 units (additional 5 percent) and 1,000 units
(additional 10 percent).

Discounts cannot be combined with promotional pricing.
